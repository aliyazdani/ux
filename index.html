Intermediate UX/UI Designer. Part-time, Remote.

Posted Jul 5, 2021

CallHero is a small, innovative team of tight-knit people working hard to help businesses manage their phone systems and front-desk staff.

We are based in Vancouver, Canada but our staff are all around the world.

Our software provides a modern, affordable, and low-friction way for small companies to build complex telephone routing, remote-staff call handling, and after-hours answering service.
We also provide rich, granular real-time analytics.

We are looking for a talented intermediate level UX/UI designer to join our team to drive our company design and help make our software beautiful and easy to use.

Your role will include

  • Talk to our customers as well as our customer success, support, and engineering teams to understand how our product brings value and collect data
    and use it to come up with functional and beautiful designs.
  • Set quarterly goals with the team and deliver results without compromising quality.
  • Use your expertise to come up with solutions and high-fi mockups that the engineering team can use to build and improve the product.

Requirements

  • 2+ years working on UX/UI of web apps with proven track-record of shipping.
  • Part-time to start (may lead to full-time) and work fully remote (any timezone is fine) at your home office or co-working space.
  • An online portfolio or examples of previous work that gives us a sense of your creative process, ability and examples of shipped work.
  • Good communicator - can consider a range of ideas and has empathy.
  • Comfortable in written and spoken english.
  • Work well independently to generate ideas, prototypes, and designs that are beautiful and functional and iterate over them with the team.
  • Comfortable conducting research and gathering data and feedback by talking to various team members and customers to generate design solutions.
